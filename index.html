// About Me

Building Systems That Never Break

An ICT engineer driven by the belief that precision, security, and automation are non-negotiable — especially where the stakes are high.

My background in Information and Communication Technology has been heavily focused on building hardened Linux environments and robust automation pipelines. I am most driven when the stakes are high — which is why I'm drawn to sectors like fintech, where a vulnerability in a script isn't just a bug, it's a financial risk.

I specialize in structuring fault-tolerant systems and terminal-based workflows, combining custom Python and Bash scripts with visual orchestration tools like Langchain. My philosophy is simple: in critical systems, risk management requires redundant, layered logic.

Beyond the terminal, I've built AI-powered platforms, designed algorithmic trading systems compliant with strict FTMO risk parameters, and architected automated threat-detection pipelines. I treat every project as a mission-critical deployment.
